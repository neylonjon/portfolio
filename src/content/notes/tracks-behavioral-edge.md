title: "Trader behavior is the only moat I trust"
summary: "Why Tracks bets on accountability, not signal."
date: 2026-04-14
tags: [tracks, trading, behavioral]
---

Every micro-cap signal eventually gets arbitraged. Every charting indicator gets commoditized. The one thing that stays scarce is a trader who actually follows the rules they wrote down at 9am.

Tracks treats journaling as an engineering surface, not a feature. Pre-trade plans get version-controlled. Stop-loss overrides get logged with a forced "why now" field. The dashboard shows you the gap between the plan you wrote and the trades you took — and that gap, more than any indicator, is the thing.
