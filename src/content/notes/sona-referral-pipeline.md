title: "Sona's referral pipeline is a state machine, not a CRM"
summary: "What you call something changes how you treat its edges."
date: 2026-04-28
tags: [sona-speech, healthcare, systems]
---

Hospitals don't send leads. They send referrals — and a referral has medical states, not sales states. "Triaged" is not "qualified." "Awaiting funding" is not "stalled in pipeline."

When we rebuilt Sona's intake as an explicit state machine, the transitions told us what was missing: no clinician had ever defined the boundary between "telehealth-suitable" and "needs in-person assessment." Two months of soft data, two hours of definition, and the bottleneck moved from the front-desk inbox to where the actual decision lives.
